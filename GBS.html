GBS Sports Meet

Gandaki Boarsing School is famous not only for its academic excellents but also for its extra curricular activities.GBS, every year organized its mega event for 3 days which includes different sports activities and fun fair. This year also , GBS organized its event in 2nd, 3rd and 4th of Poush. It was organized by following highlights. Day-wise Highlights
2nd & 3rd Poush
Annual Sports Day:
Starting at 10:30 AM each day, students will compete in various track and field events, showcasing discipline, teamwork, and healthy competition.
4th Poush
Educational Exhibition & Fun Fair: From 10:30 AM, visitors can explore innovative student projects, science exhibits, art displays, and interactive learning zones, followed by games and food stalls at the Fun Fair. 4th Poush Afternoon
Annual Cultural Program Beginning at 1:00 PM, the stage will come alive with music, dance, drama, and traditional performances by GBS students, reflecting Nepal's cultural richness and creative youth.
